Happiness

Sometimes, I need more than just pure, caffiene-fueled motivation to get through my hard times. One of the best solutions I've found for a lack of energy in general is to check in with myself and my health:

  • Food and Water:
    1. Have I drank water yet today?
    2. Have I eaten food yet today?
    3. Have I been too hard on myself about food lately?
  • Rest
    1. Did I sleep more than 7 hours last night?
    2. Have I been able to reset recently?
    3. Is there anythign draining me right now that I can fix?
  • Get Outside
    1. Have I seen the sun this week?
    2. Have I made time to exercise recently?
    3. Who have I had with me when I go outside?
